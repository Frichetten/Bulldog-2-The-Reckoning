Thank you for taking the time to visit our humble website


When we started out 3 years ago, we were a photography shop focused on professional canine photography. We had a great run, made a lot of friends, and pleased many happy customers. Unfortunately we began to struggle under the weight of our own size.


As you can imagine, when you are a specialist in a niche market, your value skyrockets. We could not keep up with the demand. Around this same time the "incidents" occured.


First, we were made aware of a significant data breach orchestrated by the group: "German Shepherd Hack Team". After they copied our internal database, they distributed it online.


To make matters worse, another incident occured shortly after. An unknown individual or group of individuals got back into our systems and took everything down. Law enforcement is yet to identify this second attacker.


But we here at Bulldog Industries are not known to quit! We push on! This lead us to where we are today. Shortly after the photography business crashed we leveraged our same infrastructure to build a thriving social media platform! One the likes of which the world has never seen!

We currently service over 15,000 Bullies (users)! And have over 7,400 business partners of which we sell data to.


Our pledge to you is that we will always maintain the highest level of security and privacy. Under no situation would we ever allow an unauthorized group to access your data.


Fool us once. Shame on you. Fool us twice. Shame on you!


-Winston Churchy (CEO, Bulldog Industries)
